Site Purpose

The Cyber Threats Prevention site aims to be a comprehensive resource for understanding common cyber threats and implementing effective prevention methods. It will provide information on various attack techniques (e.g., phishing, malware, viruses) and offer practical advice and strategies for protecting personal and valuable digital assets. The site will also highlight current and prevalent cyber threats to keep users informed.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: "I received a suspicious email asking for my bank details. How can I tell if it's a phishing attempt and what should I do?"
  • Scenario 2: "My computer has been acting strangely, and I'm worried it might have a virus. What are the common signs of malware infection, and how can I remove it?"
  • Scenario 1: "I use a web browsers to remember passwords. Is that a good practice?"

Color Schema

Primary Color #003366
Secondary Color #6699CC
Accent Color #FFCC00
Background Color #F5F5F5
  • Primary Color: #003366 (Dark Blue)
    • Used for: Headings (H1, H2, H3), navigation links, call-to-action buttons, and footer background. This color evokes trust, stability, and security.
  • Secondary Color: #6699CC (Medium Blue)
    • Used for: Paragraph text, accents, and borders. This lighter blue provides a good contrast with the dark blue and maintains a professional yet approachable feel.
  • Accent Color: #FFCC00 (Amber)
    • Used for: Highlighted statistics, important alerts, and interactive elements. This color adds a touch of vibrancy and draws attention to key information.
  • Background Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
    • Used for: Overall page background. This neutral color provides a clean and readable canvas for the content.
